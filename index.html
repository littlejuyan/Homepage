Biography

I am third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at State University of New York at Buffalo, advised by Prof. Siwei Lyu. Before that, I got B.Eng. and M.Eng. from Xidian University in 2016 and 2019.

My current research interest lies in digital media forensics media forensics including DeepFake detection and attribution.
